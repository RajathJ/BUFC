About Us

Overview

Bangalore United Football Club (BUFC) started in the year 1940, and has the legacy of owning many Internationals who played for the club. The club also has very strong links to its local community and Government schools conducting and managing football for them from years . We hold regular Camps for boys and Girls . Our under 12 years team is the winners of County Cup last season and 2 senior players were recently selected to play for the county . Many of our players have represented Karnataka state and Indian National team.

We are very proud of our achievements and it’s the hard work of many likeminded people connected with the club which has led to on-field success.

Many of our players have represented Karnataka state and Indian National team. BUFC players are trained under qualified Coaches who possess ‘A’ license for coaching from Asian Football Confederation and also by ex-Internationals. We also conduct clinics for players and Coaches as a part of development. BUFC Women Team are the champion of Impetus Trophy (Aug 2018) organised by Karnataka state Football association. We are the 1ST crowned CHAMPIONS of KARNATAKA STATE WOMENS FOOTBALL LEAGUE which started in 2019. Participated INDIAN WOMEN LEAGUE (IWL) 2019 in PUNJAB & 2020 in BANGALORE.
